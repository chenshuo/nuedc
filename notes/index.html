SPICE

SPICE/LTspice/NgSpice

Version 1 released in 1973, TR by Laurence W. Nagel and Donald O. Pederson (1925~2004).

The lase release from Berkeley was 3f5. It compiles and runs on Linux https://github.com/hedhyw/spice3f5.
